        Data Sources:
        Gene lists sourced from WikiPathways SPARQL

        GO gene annotations from go_bp_gene_annotations_propagated.json (pre-computed).
        Gene sets are propagated over the GO is_a/part_of closure,
        so a term carries the genes annotated to all of its descendants (the GO true-path rule).
